Sumbrary

Sumbrary is a website created by students for a course called Web Technology. The focus group of this site is every student in the world. The idea behind this website is that students are able to post their summaries of courses, books, or even solutions of i.e. math exercises. Every user on the site is able to make use of everything on the site, and will then rate the uploaded file they used on how well it is made. Getting higher feedback can result in external rewards being awarded, and lower feedback will allow the student who uploaded their file to know improvement is either necessary of would help the person out.
